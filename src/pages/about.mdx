  <h1>ABOUT US</h1>
  <p class="sub">CSARCH2 — Group 7 [S01] — 3rd Term, A.Y. 2025–2026</p>

  <p style="text-align: justify;">
    <strong style="color: var(--text);">Full Capacity: The Evolution of Computer Data Storage</strong> is
    a virtual exhibit created in partial fulfillment of the course requirements for Introduction to
    Computer Organization and Architecture 2 (CSARCH2) at De La Salle University – Manila.
  </p>

  <p style="text-align: justify;">
    The exhibit traces the history of data storage from punch cards and magnetic drums to solid-state
    drives and emerging technologies like DNA storage, highlighting the architectural principles and
    trade-offs behind each innovation.
  </p>
